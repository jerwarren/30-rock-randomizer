30 Rock Randomizer

You can "install" this page as an app in Chrome, Edge, Firefox on desktop, as well as on Android and iOS to enable automatic redirection to a random episode.

(Hulu on iOS doesn't support deep-linking, however, so iOS is limited to Amazon Prime Video.)

After installing, select Hulu or Amazon the first time; it'll remember that going forward.

The app will now show up in your applications menus, and you can launch it via quicksearch on Windows by pressing the Windows key on Windows (or CMD + space on a Mac) and typing '30 Rock'
